Welcome

MyCollegeWingMan, created by high school students
for high school students, is here to help make the college research process
smoother and less stressful. MyCollegeWingMan includes a list of 50 colleges
from each US region, the WingMan chatbot for tips and tricks, and
a quiz to help match students with a college!
